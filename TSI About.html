Our story

Signal, not spin — for two decades and counting

The Smart Investor began in 2003 as a modest print newsletter for a handful of local investors who wanted signal, not spin. Two decades on — and freshly revived in 2023 as a digital-first newsroom — that founding principle still guides everything we publish.

We cover the three things that determine outcomes for readers: the nation and the policy that shapes it, the decisions made in boardrooms and central banks, and the wider region whose markets are increasingly intertwined.

Our reporting is built on primary sources, careful analysis, and a refusal to chase hype. We write for readers who want to understand why markets move, not just that they did — from first-time investors to professionals managing institutional mandates.

Independence, for us, is a structure rather than a slogan: editorial operates separately from commercial partnerships, advertising is always labelled, and corrections are published openly. Trust is earned one accurate story at a time.
